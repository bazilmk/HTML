Members of the Band

We are a Melbourne based start up band created in January 2016. Our band has four members, who have deep love to create rock music and influence the generations to come.

Daniel Hunter

Born in 1981, I am the lead singer of our band, since I was a child I loved to sing and Freddie Mercury and Mark Knopfeller had a huge influence on my life to become a singer. My ambition is to be up there with the greatest, just like my idols.

Ryan Jackson

Born in 1990, I am the lead guitarist of our band, since a child I have been playing the guitar. Slash and Joe Satriani have been my idols, they have led me down this beautiful path and shaped my music career and ambitions.

Xavier Benjamin

Born in 1986, I am the bassist of our band, since a child I have been playing the bass. My father himself was a bassist who has had an immense influence on my career. He was never able to start off his music career, I would love to fulfill his dream.

Sebastian Flynn

Born in 1993, Drummer of the band. In High School, I started playing the drums for the high school band. From there on, till today I have been playing the drums and decided that I want this to be something bigger than just a hobby.
